Program Design Philosophy: Based on periodization principles with alternating high-intensity and recovery days. Includes progressive overload, adequate rest, and flexibility for life balance.

Running Progression

Weeks 1-2: Base building, 15-20km/week
Weeks 3-4: Introduce intervals, 20-25km/week
Weeks 5-6: Increase intensity, 25-30km/week
Weeks 7-8: Peak & taper, 20-25km/week

Final Reflection & Future Vision

Personal insights from my transformation journey and roadmap ahead

My 8-Week Transformation Story

When I started this journey on January 20th, I saw it as just another school assignment. Eight weeks later, it's become the catalyst for a complete lifestyle transformation that I know will impact the rest of my life.

The Mental Shift

The biggest surprise wasn't the physical changes—it was the mental transformation. Around week 3, exercise stopped being something I "had to do" and became something I "got to do." Missing a workout now feels like missing breakfast; it just doesn't feel right. This shift from obligation to opportunity has been revolutionary.

The numbers tell one story: 2 minutes and 42 seconds faster on my 5k, 16 more push-ups, 5kg added to my bench press. But they don't capture the full picture—the confidence I feel walking into a room, the energy that lasts throughout the day, or the pride in keeping a commitment to myself for 8 straight weeks.

Unexpected Benefits

  • • Improved relationship with food—seeing it as fuel, not enemy
  • • Better time management—had to plan days to fit workouts
  • • Stronger friendships—Mike became my training partner
  • • Academic improvement—better focus during study sessions
  • • Family inspiration—dad started running again after 10 years

Challenges Overcome

  • • Week 2: Severe DOMS almost made me quit
  • • Week 4: Plateau frustration—numbers weren't moving
  • • Week 5: Exam stress—wanted to skip workouts
  • • Week 6: Minor knee pain—had to modify running
  • • Throughout: Fighting the "I'm too tired" voice

What started as pursuing two SMART goals evolved into discovering what I'm truly capable of when I commit fully. The discipline developed through 4am wake-ups for runs and pushing through the burn of that last push-up set has already transferred to other areas of my life.

The Ripple Effect

This program didn't just change me—it inspired others. My younger brother started doing push-ups with me. My mom began her own walking routine. Three classmates asked for copies of my program. Even my hockey coach noticed the improvements in my game stamina. Fitness, I've learned, is contagious when pursued with passion.
